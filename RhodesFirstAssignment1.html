About Me

Hello! I'm Austin, a software engineer and aspiring game developer as well as an Air Force veteran from North Carolina, US. I've always loved video games more than anything and I dreamed of making my own from as early as I can remember playing them. I'm currently building my skills while working on a side scrolling game I am tentatively calling "Block Boy the Monster Slayer". Currently I am pursuing a bachelors in IT at American Military University and plan to transfer to a Computer Science and Game Development program at North Carolina State University.


On this site you can see some of the projects I've been working on. I am emmensly curious, commited and a fast learner. I love learning the technical background to software and the amazing video games I get to play.


Please feel free to contact me with any questions or comments you may have!
